Shoulder Conditions We Treat

From acute sports injuries to chronic degenerative conditions — our specialists provide comprehensive care for every shoulder concern.

Rotator Cuff Injuries

Partial and full-thickness tears, tendinopathy, and impingement. Our rehabilitation protocols restore strength and mobility safely.

Frozen Shoulder

Adhesive capsulitis can severely limit movement. We use targeted manual therapy and progressive exercise to regain full range of motion.

Shoulder Impingement

Pinching of the rotator cuff tendons causes pain and weakness. We identify the root cause and design a correction plan that works.

Labrum Tears

SLAP tears and Bankart lesions require precise diagnosis. Our post-surgical and conservative rehab pathways are built on current evidence.

Tendinitis & Bursitis

Inflammation around the shoulder joint can be debilitating. We combine hands-on therapy with load management for lasting relief.

Sports Injuries

Dislocations, separations, and overuse injuries in athletes. We focus on safe return-to-play with strength and conditioning support.
