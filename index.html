What We Treat

Specialized Care for Every Condition

From acute sports injuries to chronic pain, our clinic offers targeted treatments for a wide range of shoulder conditions.

Rotator Cuff Injuries

Comprehensive repair and rehabilitation for tears and strains in the rotator cuff muscles, restoring full range of motion.

Frozen Shoulder

Targeted physiotherapy and pain management techniques to break the cycle of stiffness and discomfort.

Shoulder Impingement

Diagnostic assessments and corrective exercises to relieve pressure and restore smooth joint movement.

Labrum Tears

Advanced therapeutic interventions to stabilize the shoulder joint following a labral tear.

Tendinitis

Evidence-based treatments to reduce inflammation and strengthen tendons affected by overuse.

Sports Injuries

Rapid response and rehab protocols designed for athletes to ensure a safe and fast return to play.

Our Process

Your Path to Recovery

A structured, transparent, and supportive journey from your first visit to full recovery.

1. Consultation

Comprehensive discussion of your symptoms, medical history, and lifestyle goals.

2. Diagnosis

Physical examinations and imaging to identify the specific nature of your shoulder condition.

3. Personalized Treatment

Implementation of targeted therapies, pain management, or surgical intervention if necessary.

4. Rehabilitation

Guided physiotherapy sessions to rebuild strength, flexibility, and joint stability.

5. Recovery

Final assessments and maintenance strategies to ensure long-term health and prevent re-injury.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your visit and treatment process.

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, any relevant medical records or imaging results (X-rays, MRIs), comfortable clothing, and a list of any medications you are currently taking.

While a referral from your primary care physician is appreciated, it is not strictly necessary to book a consultation with our orthopedic specialists. You can contact us directly to schedule an appointment.

Recovery time varies greatly depending on the injury type and severity. Minor tendinitis might resolve in 4-6 weeks, while post-surgical rotator cuff repair can take 4-6 months. We provide a personalized timeline after your diagnosis.

We accept most major insurance plans. Coverage depends on your specific policy and the treatment required. Our administrative team will help verify your benefits before you begin your rehabilitation program.
