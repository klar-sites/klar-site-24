Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive diagnostic and treatment services for a wide range of shoulder conditions.

Rotator Cuff Injuries

Expert repair and rehabilitation for tears and strains in the rotator cuff tendons.

Frozen Shoulder

Targeted therapies to reduce stiffness and restore range of motion in adhesive capsulitis.

Shoulder Impingement

Alleviating pain and pinching sensations through posture correction and targeted exercises.

Labrum Tears

Advanced diagnostic and surgical solutions for SLAP tears and labral instability.

Tendinitis

Inflammation management and strengthening protocols for biceps and shoulder tendons.

Sports Injuries

Performance-focused recovery for acute athletic shoulder trauma and overuse injuries.

Our Treatment Process

A clear, structured path from initial pain to full recovery.

1. Consultation

A thorough discussion of your symptoms, medical history, and lifestyle goals to understand your needs.

2. Diagnosis

Advanced imaging and physical assessments to pinpoint the exact root cause of your shoulder pain.

3. Personalized Plan

Development of a tailored treatment strategy, whether it requires surgical intervention or conservative care.

4. Rehabilitation

Hands-on physiotherapy and guided exercises to restore strength, stability, and mobility.

5. Recovery

Final phase conditioning and preventative education to ensure long-term shoulder health.
