Getting Here

📍 Map — 12 Orchard Lane, London W1U 3AB

Public Transport

We're a 5-minute walk from Bond Street station (Central & Jubilee lines) and 7 minutes from Oxford Circus. Several bus routes stop on Orchard Lane.

Parking

Limited on-street parking is available on Orchard Lane (pay-by-phone). There is a secure multi-storey car park at Cavendish Square, a 3-minute walk away.

Accessibility

Our clinic is fully step-free with a ground-floor entrance, wide doorways, and an accessible treatment room. If you have specific access needs, please let us know when booking.
