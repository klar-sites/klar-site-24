About OrthoCore

We are a dedicated team of shoulder specialists who believe that recovery is personal — and that every patient deserves expert care delivered with warmth and compassion.

Our Story

OrthoCore was founded in 2010 by Dr. Henrik Larsson, a physiotherapist who saw a gap in how shoulder injuries were treated. Too often, patients received generic rehab protocols that failed to address the unique mechanics of the shoulder joint — or the individual needs of the person behind the injury.

Our clinic was built on a simple premise: combine the best available evidence with a genuinely human approach. From day one, we committed to longer appointment times, continuity of care, and a calm, unhurried clinical environment.

Over fifteen years, we've grown from a single-room practice into one of the most trusted shoulder rehabilitation centres in the region — but our core philosophy has never changed.

Our Philosophy

We believe recovery is a partnership — between specialist and patient, science and empathy, structure and flexibility.

Patient-Centred Care

You are not a diagnosis. You are a person with goals, fears, and a life you want to return to. Every decision we make starts with understanding what matters to you.

Clinical Excellence

We invest heavily in continuing education, peer review, and staying current with the latest research. If a treatment isn't supported by evidence, we don't use it.

Continuity & Trust

You'll see the same clinician throughout your journey. We believe therapeutic alliance — the trust between you and your therapist — is a powerful predictor of outcome.

Our Mission

To provide the highest standard of shoulder rehabilitation in an environment that feels calm, safe, and genuinely supportive — so every patient leaves not just recovered, but empowered.

A Clinic Designed for Healing

Our facility was intentionally designed to feel unlike a typical clinical environment. Soft lighting, natural materials, and generous space create an atmosphere that is calm and restorative — because we know that environment affects outcomes.

We have three private treatment rooms, a dedicated rehabilitation gym, and on-site diagnostic equipment. Every detail, from the colour palette to the furniture, was chosen to help you feel at ease.

  • Private, sound-insulated treatment rooms
  • Fully equipped rehabilitation gym
  • On-site diagnostic imaging access
  • Accessible ground-floor facility with step-free access
