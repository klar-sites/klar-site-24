About Our Clinic

Dedicated exclusively to the complexities of the shoulder, combining surgical excellence with compassionate rehabilitation.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Nordic Shoulder Clinic began as a specialized practice responding to a growing need for focused, expert care in shoulder orthopedics. We recognized that the shoulder is one of the most complex joints in the human body, and generic treatments often fall short.

Over the years, we have grown into a premier facility, bringing together top-tier orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and diagnostic specialists under one roof. Our singular focus allows us to perfect our craft and offer unparalleled expertise in both conservative and surgical shoulder management.

Today, we are proud to be a trusted destination for athletes, professionals, and individuals seeking to restore their quality of life through comprehensive shoulder care.

Our Philosophy & Mission

Our Philosophy

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the joint. True recovery requires understanding the patient's lifestyle, goals, and concerns. We prioritize conservative treatments whenever possible, reserving surgery for when it is truly necessary.

Our Mission

To restore mobility and eliminate pain through evidence-based, personalized shoulder care. We are committed to achieving the best possible outcomes for our patients, allowing them to return to their passions unhindered.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and excellence form the core of our practice. We communicate transparently, treat with empathy, and never compromise on the quality of our medical standards.
